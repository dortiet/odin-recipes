Vanilla Cupake

Dozen vanilla cupcakes takes around 40 minutes to make
  • 150g of softened butter
  • 150g of fine sugar
  • 340g of self-rising flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  1. Prehead oven to 175 degrees C. Grease the muffin pan, or line with cups/silicone baking cups
  2. Mix butter and sugar in a bowl, until its light and fluffy
  3. Stir in the eggs one at a time blending well after each.
  4. Stir in the vanilla and flour until mixed
  5. Spoon the batter into cups diving evenly
  6. Bake in the oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed.(about 18-20 minutes)
